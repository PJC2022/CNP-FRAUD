This project reviewed E-Commerce transactions from a fictional online computer retailer. With my experience in credit card fraud prevention and a deep understanding of how fraudsters attempt to manipulate CNP payment systems, I wanted to display the impact of not having a fraud prevention solution. I analyzed the data in both Microsoft Power BI and Tableau. Please feel free to navigate and test the Live Tableau Dashboard on my Tableau Public page.
